Types:
M = melee
R = ranged
E = explosive
F = fire
W = water
L = electric
G = magic
C = mechanical
A = aerial
I = ice
V = evil
S = star
. = default

Strategies:
0: fighter selected at random (also is default)
1: best to worst
2: worst to best
3: 2nd best to worst, best last
4: smart
99: real user

Rarities:
0: Unique fighter (will never replicate)
1+: Generic fighter (higher number means more common)
-1: Replicated fighter
